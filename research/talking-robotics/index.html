Talking Robotics Seminar Presentation

January 25, 2021

I recently gave a virtual seminar presentation on the state of my thesis work. This includes prior work on CC-LfD and ARC-LfD, my current work on constrained motion planning, and future human-subjects studies coming down the pipeline.

The seminar series, called Talking Robotics, is hosted by a group of students and post-docs, one of whom I met at the AAAI Doctoral Consortium. It is a great opportunity for current students, post-docs, and faculty to continue to advertise their research and network with their peers, especially in light of the COVID19 pandemic.
